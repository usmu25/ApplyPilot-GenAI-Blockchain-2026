# Skill: Cover Letter and Email Drafting

## Purpose
Generate a cover letter and email body after the CV is finalized.

## Inputs
- Finalized CV
- Opportunity details

## Steps
1. Read finalized CV.
2. Read opportunity details.
3. Generate cover letter based only on CV evidence.
4. Generate short professional email body.
5. Prepare subject line.
6. Store draft for user review.

## Rules
- Do not invent experience.
- Do not claim email has been sent.
- Do not send email automatically.
- Attach CV PDF and cover letter PDF only after /sendemail.

## Approval Needed
Yes. Email sending requires explicit user command.

## Output
- Cover letter
- Email subject
- Email body
